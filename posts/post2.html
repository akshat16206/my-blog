Idea-Capture-AI

Posted on June 24, 2025

Ever had a brilliant idea in your head and lost it before writing it down? I built a solution to that: an offline AI-powered notebook that listens, transcribes, summarizes, and stores your ideas ‚Äî all without relying on internet or API keys.

What This Project Does

  • Records your voice using a Python script
  • Transcribes the audio into text with whisper.cpp
  • Summarizes your thoughts using a local LLM (llama.cpp + Mistral 7B)
  • Saves everything in a searchable JSON notebook
  • Works 100% offline ‚Äî perfect for privacy and speed

Use Cases

  • Capturing creative ideas on the fly
  • Building a voice-based productivity journal
  • Recording lecture notes or tech insights quickly
  • Hands-free ideation for developers or thinkers
